Welcome to LIFT OFF!

-The goal of LIFT OFF is to help your Weightlifter complete a "Clean and Press".

Here's what you need to know.

-A Clean and Press is done by lifting the bar to your waist, then to your chest and finally over your head.

-To complete this, you will need to pass 10 quick time events based around the Arrow Keys on browser or the Arrow Buttons on mobile.

-Lifting the weight is performed by landing the slider inside of the coloured portion of the bar using the UP, LEFT, or RIGHT arrow keys (or buttons) as instructed.

-"Locking out" the weight is done with the DOWN arrow key to settle the weight at your waist, shoulders, and over your head.
